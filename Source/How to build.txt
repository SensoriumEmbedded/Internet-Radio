
How to Build:

  Arduino IDE, v2.3.2 was last used, newer should be fine.
  
  Libraries are included to preserve revision and modification of TFT_eSPI:
   Set sketchbook (lib) location to Source folder (contains /libraries)
  
  Additional board manager:  https://dl.espressif.com/dl/package_esp32_index.json   -or-
                             https://raw.githubusercontent.com/espressif/arduino-esp32/gh-pages/package_esp32_index.json

  Board: "ESP32 Dev Module" (near top of list)  (was NodeMCU-32S)
